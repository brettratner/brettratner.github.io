Monty Hall Simulation

Tolls Used: Javascript, HTML, CSS

The Monty Hall problem was a scenario on the she game show “Lets Make a Deal” where you selected one of three closed doors. Behind one of the doors was a prize and the other two doors had non-prize items or nothing at all. After you pick your door the host would then remove one of the doors that did not have the prize behind it and you were then left with the question if you would want to switch doors to the other unopened door or keep the one you originally choose. This simulation will run this scenario 10,000 times and then tell you the results of the percentage of which the player would have won if they kept their original door against if they would have switched. You may think that between the two remaining doors your chances of winner are 50 50, however this simulation shows that that is not the case.
